The Human Brain

The human brain is the control center of the body. It helps us think, learn, remember, solve problems, make decisions, control emotions, and coordinate our movements. Keeping the brain healthy through good sleep, regular exercise, healthy food, and learning new skills improves mental well-being and overall development.
